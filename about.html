About Us

Dedicated to preserving and promoting the beautiful art traditions of Mithila while empowering women in our community.

Our Mission

Mithila KritiKala Foundation is committed to preserving the ancient art traditions of Mithila while providing sustainable livelihood opportunities for women and girls in Madhubani, Bihar.

We believe that traditional arts are not just cultural treasures but also powerful tools for economic empowerment and community development.

  • Preserve traditional Mithila art techniques
  • Empower women through skill development
  • Create sustainable income opportunities
  • Build strong community networks

Our Founder

Vishal Kumar Thakur

Founder & Managing Trustee

With a deep passion for Mithila's cultural heritage and a vision for women's empowerment, Vishal Kumar Thakur founded this organization to create a platform for sustainable change and artistic preservation.
